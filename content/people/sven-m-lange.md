Sven leads the lab's research program. Sven completed his B.Sc. and M.Sc. in 2015 in Germany before moving to Scotland for his Ph.D. with [Sir Philip Cohen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip_Cohen_(British_biochemist)) at the MRC Protein Phosphorylation and Ubiquitylation Unit (MRC PPU) in Dundee, where he structurally characterized IRAK pseudokinases. In 2020, he then joined [Yogesh Kulathu's lab](https://www.kulathulab.org/), also at the MRC PPU, working on branched ubiquitin chains and deubiquitinases. Next, Sven joined [Alan Brown's lab](https://brown.hms.harvard.edu/) at Harvard Medical School, Boston, as a postdoc in 2022 to determine [how ubiquitinated proteins are retrieved from cilia](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2025.07.043). He was awarded a Sara Elizabeth O'Brien Trust Postdoc Fellowship in 2023.
